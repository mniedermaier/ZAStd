Play Now GitHub

Features

👑

8 Governors

Fire, Ice, Thunder, Poison, Death, Nature, Arcane, Holy — each with unique abilities and tower trees.

🏰

35 Towers

3 common towers plus 4 per governor. Chain lightning, splash, poison, stun, execute, teleport, and aura buffs.

🌐

Multiplayer

Real-time co-op via Supabase. Build mazes together, share lives, combine governor synergies.

🧩

Maze Building

Place towers to create intricate mazes. BFS pathfinding ensures enemies always find a way — make it as long as possible.

👾

11 Enemy Types

Armored, flying, healers, berserkers, splitters, and bosses every 10 waves. 40 waves to victory.

⚔️

Damage System

Physical vs magic damage. Armor, magic resist, armor debuffs. Stack synergies for devastating combos.

💰

Economy

Earn gold per wave, gain interest, collect lumber every 5 waves. 5 tech upgrades to customize your strategy.

🗺️

Multiple Maps

3 sizes and 3 layouts: Classic, Spiral, and Crossroads. Easy, Normal, Hard, and Extreme difficulty.
